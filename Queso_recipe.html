The Ultimate Sea Food Queso Recipe

Ingredients

  • Brick of Cheese (Wal-Mart)
  • 8oz Can of Green Peppers and Tomatoes
  • Bag of Small Shrimp
  • As much crab meat as desired
  • Lots of butter
  • Garlic

Instructions

  1. Firstly, cook all the seafood in a pan. This is best done by melting butter in the pan, and then stirring the seafood in at a medium temperature whilst sprinkling garlic over the top.
  2. Melt your cheese into a large pot until starting to bubble.
  3. Dump the can of peppers and tomatoes into the cheese.
  4. Stir in the cooked seafood.
  5. Enjoy with chips!
